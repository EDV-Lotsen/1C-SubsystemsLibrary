Managed form

Forms of documents ProductReceipt and ProductExpense demonstrate how to work with the various items of the form: Field, Group, Pages Group, Table, and so on. In these forms the script operations in a managed form are demonstrated. For example, the recalculation of the amount when the price or quantity are changed or obtaining the price when product is changed. The amount recalculation on the quantity change can be performed without of calling the server. On an example of the price obtaining on the product change the server function without of the form context call is demonstrated. It also demonstrates how to Include column totals in a form and display the number of rows in the tabular section on a form tab.

The radio buttons usage is demonstrated in the catalog item form of Products catalog, see the product kind. The enumeration values are set in the ChoiceList property.

Various groups use cases are demonstrated in the catalogs items forms of Products and Counterparties catalogs, as well as in the document form of a ProductExpense document.

In the list form of the MutualSettlements accumulation register the possibility of opening the form with the certain row activation in the list is demonstrated.

In the form of the ProductReceipt document the value of reference type displaying in the tabular section is demonstrated. In the SKU is displayed in the Products tabular section.

In the form of the ProductExpense document the Recalculate Command is an example of the form data conversion into the application object and vice versa.

In the form of the ProductExpense document the conditional form appearance usage and including additional columns into the data, correlated with the table, is demonstrated. For the form data, representing the Products table the AmountChanged column is added, in which the various handlers the sign is placed: whether the amount of the row differs from the production of the price by quantity. In the conditional appearance of the form the Amount field is marked with red color and bold font for such rows.

In the form of ProductExpense document the creation of a new catalog item during the document filling is demonstrated.

In the fill form of the ProductExpense document demonstrates the usage of the currently entered text in the input field without of changing the selected field. In the fill form the field for fast product search by the substring. Filter in the product list is performed while the search string entering.

In the product editing form the form items enabled state changing depending on the current data values is demonstrated.

Including commands into the context menu is demonstrated in the ProductExpense documents list form. For the table in this form into the context menu the print command is added.
