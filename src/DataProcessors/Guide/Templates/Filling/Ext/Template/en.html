Filling

The technique of providing the picking functionality is demonstrated in the form of the ProductExpense document. For the pickup of the FillForm form is used. It is opened modal in the document form command handler.

For transferring the tabular section content to the pickup form and back the temp storage with the form ID specification is used.

The pickup form have a drag and drop functionality for dragging products from the all products list into the picked products list, the Drag mechanism of the platform is used for that.
