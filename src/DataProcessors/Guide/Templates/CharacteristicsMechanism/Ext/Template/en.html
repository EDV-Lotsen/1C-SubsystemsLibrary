Characteristics mechanism

In the configuration the caracteristics usage for Products and Counterparties catalogs is set.

Types of characteristics are stored in the CharacteristicTypes chart of characteristic types. The characteristic is assigned to the metadata object in through the Purpose field.

In the demo infobase characteristics are displayed in ReportByMutualSettlements, WarehouseStockBalance reports (in "Product Balance with Characteristics" variant).

Besides the characteristics are displayed in a dynamic list of the Product with Balance Choice Form. The Product with Balance Choice Form could be opened by adding a Product into the tabular section of the Sales document.
