Contact Information

The Contact Information subsystem allows you to implement the following features:

  • Extending the list of catalog attributes by adding contact information attributes
  • Automatic filling of some address fields (country, region, and so on) based on the postal code field

These attributes can be standard (such as e-mail or phone number) or custom (such as "phone number from 6:00 PM till 0:00 AM").

The Contact Information subsystem adds the following forms and form items:

  • Contact Information Kinds form
  • World Countries form (the list of countries with their codes)
  • Various contact information fields (such as e-mail address or phone number)
