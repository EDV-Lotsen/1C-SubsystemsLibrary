E-mail Operations

The E-mail Operations subsystem allows you to implement the following features:

  • Specifying e-mail account settings
  • Sending e-mail messages

The E-mail Operations subsystem adds the following forms and form items:

  • E-mail Accounts form
  • The system e-mail account (a predefined item in the E-mail Accounts form)
  • Send Message form (allows sending e-mail messages)
