The Purchasing section automates purchasing business processes. Automatic document posting updates inventory balances and creates general journal transactions.

The standard purchasing transaction workflow is Purchase order → Purchase invoice → Invoice payment.

The Purchasing section contains the following documents:

  • Purchase order. Initiates a purchase workflow. AccountingSuite tracks received and invoiced items against purchase orders.
  • Purchase invoice. Records your liability to a vendor and updates inventory balances. You can also use purchase invoices for entering beginning vendor balances.
  • Purchase return. Records an item return to a vendor. You can also use purchase returns for entering beginning vendor balances.
  • Cash purchase. Combines a purchase invoice and an invoice payment into a single transaction.
     

The Purchasing section contains the following report:

  • A/R aging. Accounts payable aging report.
