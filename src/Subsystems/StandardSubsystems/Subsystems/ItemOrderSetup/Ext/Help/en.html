For a number of objects, order of items in lists can be specified. To arrange the items, use the Up and Down buttons located on the form command panel. In hierarchical and subordinate lists, items can be moved only within the limits of a group.

In some cases, the Up and Down buttons are disabled and list items can not be arranged:

  • when the list is filtered so that some of the list items are hidden;
  • when the list is sorted by any other attribute, for example, by item name.

In these cases, a warning is displayed whenever you click Up or Down.
