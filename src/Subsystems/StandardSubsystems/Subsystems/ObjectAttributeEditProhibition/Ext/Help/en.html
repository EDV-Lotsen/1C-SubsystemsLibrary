This application is used to protect the key object attributes (crucial parameters determining the nature of an object) from editing, after they are filled in initially. Generally, key attributes of a object influence behavior of other object (example: document posting). Unadvised edits of such attributes can result in discrepancies between the accounting data.

Examples of key attributes: the Currency attribute of the Cash desks catalog that determines the balance currency for given cash desk; the Is service attribute of the Products and services catalog that influences posting of documents related to given products or services. Once the balance is registered for a cash desk, or document related to specific products are registered, these attributes can not be edited any more.
