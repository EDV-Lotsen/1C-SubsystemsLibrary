The Sales section automates sales business processes. Automatic posting of sales documents updates inventory balances and creates general journal transactions.

The standard sales transaction workflow is Sales order → Sales invoice → Cash receipt.

The Sales section contains the following documents:

  • Sales order. Initiates a sales workflow. AccountingSuite tracks shipped and invoiced items against sales orders.
  • Sales invoice. Records a customer's liability to you and your income from the transaction. Sales invoices calculate cost of goods sold (COGS), and update inventory balances. You can use also sales invoices for entering beginning customer balances.
  • Sales return. Records an item return by a customer. You can also use sales returns for recording credit memos and for entering beginning customer balances.
  • Cash sale. Combines a sales invoice and a cash receipt into a single transaction.
     

The Sales section contains the following lists:

  • Price list. Stores daily item prices that are automatically pulled to sales documents.
  • Sales tax codes. Stores sales taxes.
     

The Sales section contains the following report:

  • A/R aging. Accounts receivable aging report.
