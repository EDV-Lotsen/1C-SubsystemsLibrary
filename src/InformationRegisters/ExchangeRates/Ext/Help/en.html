The Exchange rates list stores currency exchange rates. When you enter a document that includes foreign currency transactions, the system automatically looks up an exchange rate for the specified date and enters the value into the document.

The Exchange rates list is only available if the Multicurrency check box is selected in Accounting settings.

To add an exchange rate

  1. In the Lists section, click Exchange rates and then click Create.
  2. Enter the date and the exchange rate.
  3. Click Write and close.
