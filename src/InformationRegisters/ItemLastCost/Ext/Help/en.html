The Item last cost list stores last cost of items that were purchased. The costs are automatically taken from posted purchase documents, and you also have the option to edit the list manually.

When you add items to purchase documents, their costs are prefilled with the last costs.  

To add an item last cost

  1. In the Warehouse section, click Item last costs and then click Create.
  2. Enter the item and the cost.
  3. Click Write and close.
