This list is used to display the latest information on association between users, performer roles, and addressing objects. This information is used by the business process logic to assign tasks to specific users.

Role addressing list examples:

Performer role User Explanation
Room supervisor S. Smith Tasks assigned to the Room supervisor role will be added to Smith's personal task list.
Room supervisor J. Johnson Tasks assigned to the Room supervisor role will be added to Johnson's personal task list.
Manager R. Roberts Tasks assigned to the manager will be added to Roberts' personal task list.

However, in certain cases association between role and users is not precise and performers cannot be determined. In these cases, you need to explicitly specify the primary and additional addressing objects. Example:
