This form is used to view (but not edit) the basic task attributes. Here you can view attributes that are common for all task types, such as task name, number, or creation date. In the Addressing attribute group, task performers and their roles, as well as primary and additional addressing objects, are displayed. In the Business process attribute group, numbers and dates of business processes linked to the tasks, as well as current route points, are displayed. In the Task completion results attribute group, task completion flags and task completion dates are displayed (for completed tasks only).
