Description of access group folder properties.

To facilitate management of multiple access groups with similar rights restrictions and avoid confusion, you can consolidate similar access groups into folders.

Access group folders can be organized by a variety of criteria.

Creating access group folders

  • Enter folder Description;
  • To make a folder subordinate to a Parent group (folder), select a folder from list and click Select, or double-click the parent folder.
