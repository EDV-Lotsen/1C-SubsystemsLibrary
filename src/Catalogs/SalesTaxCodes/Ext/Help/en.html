The Sales tax codes list stores sales taxes.

The Sales tax codes list is only available if the Sales tax (US) check box is selected in Accounting settings. 

To add a sales tax

  1. In the Sales section, click Sales tax codes, and then click Create.
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • Description. The unique sales tax code. It helps you quickly select the sales tax in documents.
    • Tax rate. The combined sales tax rate (state + county + city).
    • Tax agency. The tax agency.
  3. Click Write and close.
